Nuts and Bolts

Commissioning a piece of art by Adrian involves a site visit and consultation. During this consultation a creative brief discussed to ensure both parties are clear about the expectations and design of the work of art. The creative brief may cover off details like dimensions, colours, placement and perspective of the sculpture. Also timelines and pricing may be discussed at this point in time.
